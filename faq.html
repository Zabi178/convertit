Frequently Asked Questions

Is Convertit really free?

Yes. Convertit is freeware by ZabiTech. No ads inside the app, no watermarks, no limits. We plan to keep the core free forever.

Does it work offline?

100%. All conversions use FFmpeg and Qt on your PC. No files are uploaded.

Why do I need FFmpeg?

FFmpeg handles video and audio codecs. Install it once, Convertit detects it automatically. Image and PDF tools work without it.

Is it safe?

Yes. The installer is built with Inno Setup, code is open on GitHub, and it does not collect data.

What formats are supported?

Video: MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, WEBM, GIF. Audio: MP3, WAV, AAC, FLAC, OGG, M4A. Images: PNG, JPG, JPEG, WEBP, BMP, TIFF. PDF: merge, images to PDF, PDF to PNG.

Will you add Mac or Linux?

Windows is the focus for 1.0. Mac and Linux builds are planned for 2026.
